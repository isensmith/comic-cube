Comic-Cube app users manage their pull lists for you

The Comic-Cube app is free for download and provides the list of upcoming new comic books weekly. Readers can use the app to select which books they want in their box each week by coming to your store, making a box with you, and getting a Comic-Cube account. From that point on, anything they "box" with the app will be information instantly available to you.

New Comic Book Day

Anyone can get the Comic-Cube app for Free to see the new comics list weekly. We integrate as much data about the new comic books as we can find from reliable sources online, including the new Marvel API which provides additional information and images about each new book under the Marvel banner.

Sell More Comic Books

Using Comic-Cube at your store gives your readers a convenient connection to their pull list 24 hours a day. Since they can see everything that's coming out in advance, they're more likely to box more comics. Comic-Cube can help turn a curious customer into a regular one, and a casual reader into an avid one.

Track your subscriptions

As a store owner, your Comic-Cube account provides you with streamlined reports of the pull lists of your Comic-Cube users. Reports can be emailed to you or exported in a variety of formats. Comic-Cube is designed to be simple and 100% stress-free to incorporate into your current system.

  • "How come it's free?" you might ask...

    We built Comic-Cube because we really love the medium of the physical comic book and we just don't want to see it disappear.

    Today, there are a lot of services to help people buy digital comics, and that's great, but we love our local comic book stores. We wanted to make something to help them bring in and keep new customers and sell more comic books.

    That's why there are no fees for the comic book seller's side of Comic-Cube. The only fee is for the Comic-Cube app users who want an account linked to your store, and that's just $2.99, the price of one cheap comic.
